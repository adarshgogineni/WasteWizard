Frequently Asked Questions:

Some commonly recyclable items include cardboard, paper, certain plastic, glass, etc.
Recyclables have symbols on them that contain numbers in the middle. Different regions are able to recycle different numbers. 1 and 2s are very commonly recycled.
Recycling very often uses less energy that creating the items from scratch using raw materials.
Even if you do recycle something, be sure to clean it out thoroughly because often recycling centers do not have the resources to clean them out properly and will still through items into landfills if they are not clean enough to be recycled. This leads to many recyclable items still ending up in landfills.
Consumers are the ones placing these items into te recycling. Being aware of how your actions impact their processing and repurposing is vital to making the recyling processs better.
